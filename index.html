Wonderful house in Uccle

Avenue Montjoie 97, 1180 Uccle

Built in 1945, renovated in 2021

Available from June 2021

living area 135m² + garden 180m²

2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, 1 toilet

840.000€

In the heart of Uccle, in an absolute calm and an idyllic green place, 3-min walk from the Bois de la Cambre, an exceptional house of the 1900 preserved by a high quality renovation - At the first level of a bright living room with fireplace opening onto a garden of +/- 180m² facing South - Fully equipped American kitchen - Upstairs 2 large bedrooms including the Master suite (20m² & 17m²) and bathroom. On the lower level an office or workshop space - Laundry room and Wine cellar.
